About us

Uni Clubs Hub It is a comprehensive event management system for university student clubs, allowing clubs to easily create, manage and promote events, and providing students with a seamless platform to explore events, register and participate in various activities on campus. With user and administrator functions, it makes it easy to organize events, enhancing student engagement and increasing the visibility of student activities on campus.
